About Me

Hello! I'm Rupesh Sardar, a dedicated Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for crafting innovative and user-friendly web solutions. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology (B.Sc IT) and have further specialized in web development through comprehensive certification courses. My journey in web development has equipped me with a robust skill set in both front-end and back-end technologies. On the front end, I excel in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and React. On the back end, my expertise extends to server-side technologies such as Python, Django, and SQL databases, allowing me to build scalable and efficient applications. In addition to my technical skills, I am certified in Full Stack Web Development from IT Vedant. This certification has refined my abilities and kept me updated with industry best practices. Driven by a passion for problem-solving and a commitment to delivering high-quality solutions, I am dedicated to creating impactful and engaging digital experiences. If you’re looking for a skilled and enthusiastic developer ready to bring your ideas to life, let’s connect!
